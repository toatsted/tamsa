Get Involved in TAMSA

How can I help?

  1. SIGN UP ONLINE
    • Get regular email updates and action alerts by signing up with the TAMSA email alert system. If you work for a school district, please use a PERSONAL email
    • Follow us on Facebook
    • Follow us on Twitter
  2. SPREAD THE WORD
    • Ask your family members, friends, and colleagues to join TAMSA.
    • Ask your local PTA to include an overview of TAMSA in their next newsletter. WORD format PDF format
  3. CONTACT TEA, SBOE and LEGISTALORS (scroll down for contact info)
    • Voice your concern to the Texas Education Agency (TEA), your State Board of Education (SBOE) Representative and your State Legislators regarding meaningful student assessment.
    • Please ask them to stop the wasteful expenditures on high stakes testing and allow for more productive classroom instruction time.
    • Share your personal stories with education leaders and your elected representatives, and let them know that testing reform legislation must be implemented quickly and smoothly without negatively affecting students.
  4. CONSIDER DONATING TO TAMSA

    Your support will enable TAMSA to continue its mission to improve putlbic education in Texas through the use of menaingful and effective student assessments that allow for more productive classroom instruction and more efficient use of public funds.

    Contributions to TAMSA are not tax deductible as charitable contributions, nore are they deductible as ordinary and necessary business expenses due to TAMSA's efforts to influence legislation. Nevertheless, we greatly appreciate your contribution, which will help TAMSA as it advocates in Austin for our children and their schools.
